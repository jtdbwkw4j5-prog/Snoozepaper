Pricing and Future Access

SnoozePaper is free to browse while in development. We may offer optional paid tiers in the future to support the project. The proposed tiers below are for information only and are not active. No payment information is being collected at this time.

Proposed Tiers

  • Basic News Summaries: Access to general news ledgers, proposed at under $1 per month.
  • Sports or Specialized Coverage: Additional ledgers for sports or specialized topics, proposed around $3 per month.
  • Full Archive Access: Unlimited browsing of the entire ledger archive, proposed around $5 per month.

These tiers and prices are subject to change and will only be activated with clear notice and user consent.
