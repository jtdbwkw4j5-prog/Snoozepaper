Corrections Policy

SnoozePaper aims to be accurate and transparent. When mistakes occur, we correct them plainly and openly. Corrections identify what changed and when. They do not hide the original error unless legally required.

If a summary is materially wrong, it is corrected or removed. We value accuracy over speed.
