About SnoozePaper

SnoozePaper is an ultra‑boring, fact‑only news ledger. It exists for readers who want the record without the performance. We log who, what, when, where and sources — and little else. No outrage, no personal brand and no algorithmic rage‑bait.

Why We Exist

Many news platforms emphasize engagement, reaction and interpretation. SnoozePaper takes the opposite approach. By constraining what we include and how we write, we aim to reduce emotional manipulation and give you space to think. Read tonight. Decide tomorrow.

Taglines We Use

  • Read Tonight. Decide Tomorrow.
  • No Algorithm. No Outrage.
  • Not Curated to Provoke.
  • We Report. You Interpret.
  • A Simple Log for Complicated Minds.

What We Are Not

SnoozePaper is not a hot‑take site, a Substack personality brand, a political movement or a conspiracy‑adjacent outlet. It is not a replacement for journalism or a fact‑checking authority. It is simply a quiet public record.
